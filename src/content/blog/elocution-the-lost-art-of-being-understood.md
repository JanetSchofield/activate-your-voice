Elocution is the skill of **articulate, expressive, and deliberate speech**. It involves:

**Pronunciation**: Speaking clearly, so your words land with precision.

**Articulation**: Using the muscles of the mouth and face effectively to shape sound.

**Pacing**: Knowing when to pause, slow down, or speed up for impact.

**Tone &amp; Emphasis**: Adding colour and meaning to your words through vocal variety.

**Projection**: Speaking with strength and presence, without shouting.
