Janet is a fully qualified and highly professional speech and drama teacher, with more than 30 years of teaching experience.

She has worked with various professionals, businesses, and corporate companies all over the world, helping them develop their speech and communication skills.

Her passion for drama and theatre is key in helping others achieve their own success. What began as a love of performing for Janet, as a member of the Manchester Youth Theatre, has led to professional acting training, roles in various productions and TV appearances.

This background is an ideal platform from which to give insight and understanding into the performing world. Over the years, Janet has helped clients develop their speech, communication and performing skills with excellent results.

"She endeavours to inspire you to learn and develop your talents to become the best that you can be."

## Qualifications

- Licentiate of Speech and Drama Teaching Diploma (LLCM-TD) University of West London
- Associate in Speech and Drama Teaching Diploma (ALCM-TD) University of West London
- B.Ed. University of Lancaster
- Grade 8 Speech and Drama Guildhall
- LAMDA Gold Medal in Verse and Prose
- LAMDA Silver Medal in Acting
- Society of Teachers of Speech and Drama Member
- Spotlight Member
