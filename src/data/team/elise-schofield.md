Elise is an accomplished Actor, Radio Presenter and Broadcast Journalist. She trained at the prestigious Guildford School of Acting, where she achieved her Masters, and holds a high-level degree in Drama and Theatre from the University of Kent. Elise also achieved a Distinction in Grade 8 Speech and Drama from the University of West London.

As a coach, Elise is passionate about helping others improve their communication skills, particularly those pursuing careers in acting or entertainment. She offers support in audition preparation, character work, script analysis, and voice development. She works closely with individuals to help them achieve their goals and reach their full potential.

Driven by her love for the arts Elise is passionate about using her experience and expertise to make a meaningful impact, supporting others in achieving their personal and professional goals.

## Qualifications

- MA in Acting, Guildford School of Acting
- BA (Hons) Drama and Theatre, University of Kent
- Grade 8 Distinction Speech and Drama, London College of Music
- Equity Member
- Spotlight Member
